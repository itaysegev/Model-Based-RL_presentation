Why Learn a Model?

  • Efficiency → Can simulate interactions internally
  • Control and Planning → More informed decision-making and the ability to foresee and avoid potential pitfalls.
  • Flexibility → Models enable the agent to adapt to changes in the environment by reusing the learned model for different tasks
